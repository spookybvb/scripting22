About

Maggie is a first-year student in the Multimedia Program. Maggie always wanted to go to college and do something with art, which is why she chose Multimedia. She loved art from a very young age. In her early teens, she found a love for creating art with Copic Markers and Prismacolor. Maggie likes to draw anime and realism. She loves drawing her favourite bands such as Black Veil Brides and Motionless in White. After college Maggie wants to become a tattoo artist she has always had a thing for tattoos and wants to be tatted up herself.

Personality

When Maggie is not drawing she is usually watching horror movies or is hanging out with her pets. Maggie has four pets one dog and three cats Gizmo(1st pic), Scrappy(2nd pic), Icky(3rd pic), and Ozzy Pawsbourne(4th pic). Maggie is a huge horror fan she loves anything and everything that is spooky her all time favourite horror movies are The Chuckys, The conjurings, The Devil Rejects, It(2019), and Shaun of the Dead. She has loved horror movies ever since she was a little girl. Maggie also loves listening to music she has posters all over her room of her favourite bands mostly Black Veil Brides posters.

Interests

One of Maggie's favourite things to do is going to rock and metal concerts. Some of her favourite concerts she's been to are The Trinity Of Terror(Ice Nine Kills, Motionless in White, and Black Veil Brides) Shinedown, Foo Fighters, Theory of a Deadman, and Buckcherry. She loves going to the front of the stage and rocking out and loves getting a new band shirt at concerts. Maggie goes to The Grey Eagle and Casino located in Calgary, Alberta for concerts. The Grey Eagle is an awesome place to go to concerts and it's a really nice place to stay over night.
